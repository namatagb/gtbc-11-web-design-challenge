Latitude vs Wind Speed

The visualization shows here the relationship between latitude and wind speed. With the exception of some outliers as the data increases, moving further away from the equator, the data in general does not depict neither a positive nor a negative relationship between latitude, vertical proximity to the equator, and wind speed.
